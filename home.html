What is WPC?

WPC the Material
of the Future

Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) is an innovative and eco-friendly material made by combining wood fibers and thermoplastics. It offers the natural beauty of wood with the durability and low maintenance of plastic—making it an ideal choice for modern flooring, decking, cladding, and decorative applications both indoors and outdoors.

Why Choose WPC?

The Benefits of Using WPC

Weather-Resistant

WPC is designed to withstand the challenging conditions of the Philippine climate. Whether it's intense sunlight, heavy rains, or high humidity levels, WPC performs exceptionally well without fading, swelling, or deteriorating. Its moisture-resistant properties make it ideal for outdoor applications like decking, cladding, and fencing—ensuring long-term beauty and strength.

Low Maintenance

Unlike traditional wood, WPC does not require sanding, staining, sealing, or repainting. A simple occasional wash with water and mild soap is all it takes to keep it looking like new. This means you save both time and money on upkeep, making WPC a convenient choice for busy homeowners, property developers, and business owners.

Weather-Resistant

WPC is a sustainable building material made from a blend of recycled wood fibers and thermoplastics. It reduces the need for deforestation and helps divert plastic waste from landfills. By choosing WPC, you’re making a responsible choice that supports environmental conservation without sacrificing performance or style.

Weather-Resistant

WPC offers superior durability over traditional timber. It’s resistant to rotting, warping, splintering, and insect damage—ensuring it holds up well even in high-traffic or high-moisture areas. This long service life makes WPC a cost-effective solution that adds lasting value to your property.

Weather-Resistant

WPC products come in a broad selection of colors, grains, and finishes that mimic the natural look of wood while offering modern design flexibility. Whether you want a sleek contemporary look or a warm rustic feel, WPC can adapt to your aesthetic vision. It’s suitable for indoor and outdoor use—from flooring and wall panels to ceilings and decorative features.
